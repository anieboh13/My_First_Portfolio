The Secret Saturdays

An adventuresome family of experts in the field of creatures known as cryptids attempts to protect their secrets and powers from the forces of evil.

The Cast

Zak Saturday

Zak Saturday is the main protagonist of the series, the son of Drew Saturday and Doc Saturday, and the most recent reincarnation of the cryptid Kur.

Doc Saturday

Solomon "Doc" Saturday is one of the main protagonists of The Secret Saturdays. He is a member of the Secret Scientists, the father of Zak and husband to Drew, and is the adoptive father of Zon, Fiskerton, and Komodo.

Drew Saturday

Drew Saturday is one of the main protagonists of The Secret Saturdays. The wife of Doc and mother to Zak, she is a member of the Secret Scientists and one of the world's leading experts in the pseudo-science of cryptozoology.

Fiskerton Saturday

Fiskerton Saturday is one of the main protagonists of The Secret Saturdays, a Lemurian, and he is the adoptive son of Drew and Doc Saturday as well as Zak's older adoptive brother.

Doyle Blackwell

Doyle Blackwell is a major supporting character in The Secret Saturdays. Originally introduced as an antagonist and Leonidas Van Rook's apprentice, he was later revealed to be Drew's long-lost brother and he now works alongside the Saturdays.
